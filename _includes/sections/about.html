About

I am a PhD candidate in Polymer Chemistry at the University of Connecticut, working in the {{ site.lab_name }} under the supervision of {{ site.advisor }}. My research focuses on developing advanced polymer materials, with particular emphasis on graphene-based composites and coatings.
