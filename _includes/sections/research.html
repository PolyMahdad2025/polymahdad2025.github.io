Research

Graphene Composites

Developing polymer nanocomposites incorporating graphene and graphene derivatives to enhance mechanical, thermal, and electrical properties. Investigating dispersion techniques and interfacial interactions for optimized performance.

Graphene-Based Coatings

Formulating advanced coating systems utilizing graphene to improve corrosion resistance, barrier properties, and durability. Exploring applications in protective coatings for various industrial substrates.

Polymer Synthesis

Synthesizing novel polymeric materials including polyurethane foams and acrylic systems. Focus on developing sustainable formulations and understanding structure-property relationships through comprehensive characterization.
